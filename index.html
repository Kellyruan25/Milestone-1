Women in the STEM field

Throughout history, there has been a lack of women who work in the STEM field.

Reasons:

  • Gender Stereotpes, Sexism, Discrimination
  • Education Disadvantages
  • Lack of Female Role Models for Women
  • Male Dominated STEM occupations
  • Confidence Gap
  • Social Norms
  • Wage Gap

Effects:

Women only make up 28% of the STEM field. Only 38% of women who major in the computer science work in the computer science field. Only 24% of women who major in engineering work in the engineering field. Some STEM occupations have a strongly gender segregated work place.

Possible Solutions:

  • More female role models
  • Improving the STEM education system and teaching system for women
  • Encouraging young women to join STEM programs and learning opportunities
  • Support women in defying stereotypes
  • Work with industries to provide meaningful internships for women
