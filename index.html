Women in the STEM field

Throughout history, there has been a lack of women who work in the STEM field.

Reasons:

  • Gender Stereotpes, Sexism, Discrimination
  • Education Disadvantages
  • Lack of Female Role Models for Women
  • Male Dominated STEM occupations
  • Confidence Gap
  • Social Norms
  • Wage Gap
Previous Next
Effects:

Women only make up 28% of the STEM field. Only 38% of women who major in the computer science work in the computer science field. Only 24% of women who major in engineering work in the engineering field.

Possible Solutions:
  • More female role models
  • Improving the STEM education system for women
